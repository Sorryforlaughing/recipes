Triple Ginger Cookies(from Allrecipes)12Êtb (3/4 cup) butter1Êcup packed brown sugar1Êegg1/4Êcup molasses2 1/4Êcups all purpose flour2Êtsp powdered ginger2Êtsp baking soda1/2Êtsp salt1 1/2Êtb minced fresh ginger1/2Êcup diced crystallized ginger
In a stand mixer, cream the butter and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the egg and molasses. 

In a separate bowl, combine the flour, powdered ginger, baking soda and salt, then add to the butter mixture until well combined. 

Stir in the fresh and crystallized gingers by hand. Refrigerate the dough for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
Preheat the oven to 350F and place 1-inch balls of dough 2 inches apart on parchment-lined baking sheets. Bake 14 minutes, let cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, and cool completely on a wire rack.
